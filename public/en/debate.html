Student debate municipal elections Enschede

In cooperation with DAS and Taste, Fact has organized a student debate on the 14th of March in the council chamber of the town hall in Enschede. The debate has also been recorded. At this debate, six parties were present: Burger Belangen Enschede, CDA, D66, Volt en VVD. These parties have been given a number of theses during the debate, that either have a connection to students, or are about the municipality of Enschede in general.
